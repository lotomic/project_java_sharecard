Application description:
shareCard Demo

shareCard application creates mobile (digital) personalized business card that is easy for using and sharing.
Any user can create a free business card and connect it with his Timeline.
On his Timeline user can add any personal or business data related to his life. 
Fast sharing and search provide simple and efficient way of finding desired data.
